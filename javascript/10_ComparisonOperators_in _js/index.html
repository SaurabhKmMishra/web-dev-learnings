Comparison Operators

== equal to (loose equality) [it does implicit i.e, automatic conversion]

=== equal value and equal type(Strict equality) [you have to do explicit i.e, manual conversion to compare diffn. datatype values.]

**NOTE -> That is why Strict comparison is prefered over loose comparison so that we are aware about the change done by us in datatypes.

!= not equal

!== not equal value or not equal type (strict)

> greater than

< less than

>= greater than or equal to

<= less than or equal to
